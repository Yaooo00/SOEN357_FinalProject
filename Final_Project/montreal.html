Travel Recommendations

  • Duration: 3-4 days
  • Route: Begin at Old Montreal, visit Notre-Dame Basilica, explore the Montreal Museum of Fine Arts, enjoy a day at Mount Royal, and finish with a night at the lively Quartier des Spectacles.
  • Must-See Attractions: Saint Joseph's Oratory, Montreal Biodome, Jean-Talon Market, and the Plateau Mont-Royal for a taste of local life.
  • Best Time to Visit: Summer for festivals or fall for beautiful autumn colors.
