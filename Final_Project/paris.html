Welcome to Paris

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its picturesque 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

2024 Paris Olympics

The 2024 Paris Olympics will be a major event, showcasing top athletes from around the world. Key venues include the Stade de France for the opening and closing ceremonies, and new facilities across the city. It's a great time to experience the city's vibrant atmosphere and join in the global celebration of sport.

Travel Recommendations

  • Duration: 4-7 days
  • Route: Start with the Louvre, then explore the Eiffel Tower, stroll along the Champs-Élysées, visit the Montmartre district, and end with a day trip to Versailles.
  • Must-See Attractions: Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Sacré-Cœur, and Luxembourg Gardens.
  • Best Time to Visit: Late spring or early autumn, coinciding with the Olympics for a festive experience.
